EMEA: etisalat by e& Standardizes on Red Hat for Greater Innovation, Consistency and Scalability Across Hybrid Cloud ntrnolse Tue, 10/10/2023 - 14:18 Red Hat, Inc., the world's leading provider of open source solutions, today announced that etisalat by e&, telecom arm of e& (formerly known as Etisalat Group), has chosen to standardize on Red Hat technologies across its network and IT at cloud scale.
